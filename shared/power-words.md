## POWER WORD COMBINATIONS

### For SEO titles
```
[Number] + [Power Word] + [Keyword] + [Curiosity Hook]
[How] + [Power Word] + [Keyword] + [Specific Result]
[Warning/Avoid] + [Mistake] + [Keyword] + [Consequence]
```

**Examples:**
- "5 Proven [Keyword] Strategies That Actually Work in 2026"
- "How We Used a Simple [Keyword] Trick to 3× Our Traffic"
- "Stop Making These 7 [Keyword] Mistakes (Costing You Rankings)"

### For H2 headings
```
[Power Word] + [Sub-topic] + [Benefit]
[Question] + [Keyword context] + [Answer promise]
```

**Examples:**
- "The Simple [Keyword] Hack That Takes 5 Minutes"
- "Why Most People Get [Keyword] Wrong (And How to Fix It)"
- "Our Exact [Keyword] Workflow: Step-by-Step"

## SENTIMENT POLARITY

### Positive sentiment words
best, great, amazing, incredible, perfect, ultimate, love, proven, guaranteed, successful, powerful, effective, brilliant, genius, breakthrough, award-winning, top-rated, #1, leading

### Negative sentiment words (often higher CTR)
worst, avoid, stop, never, don't, mistake, fail, failure, wrong, bad, terrible, awful, dangerous, risk, warning, scam, lie, myth, truth about, hidden, what they don't tell you

### Sentiment rule for RankWise
- Every SEO title must lean positive OR negative — never neutral
- Negative titles: higher CTR on social media, controversy, fear-based
- Positive titles: better in SERP for informational/commercial queries
- Test both variants if possible
